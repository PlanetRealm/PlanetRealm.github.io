Imagine walking around a bunch of small planets solving riddles and puzzles, uncovering secrets as you go. That's our main idea, we want to build a first-person puzzle game in a low-poly, cartoonish looking world full of tiny planets that you can explore!

The game is going to be narrative driven, but the story has been evolving a lot with time. It's still being developed and polished.


Since this is our first game we didn't want to bite more than we could chew and decided to first develop the starting stage of the game, consisting of 4 playable planets. It's available as a demo for you to try! Take it as a proof of concept, the alpha version. Download link above! We will now spend some time polishing it further before continuing developing the rest of the game.

There's a lot of work left before we get to the game we're aiming for, but we're hoping that by climbing our way through Unity's learning curve, the progress is gonna get faster and faster. We still need to create assets for later stages of the game and finish with story-writing, and being restricted by working only in our spare time, it's a bit hard to talk about definitive deadlines. Our goal is to create a good game that we ourselves will enjoy playing, not to rush half finished product out there.

Once we're done, we first plan to release the game for PC, Android and iOS. These are also the platforms the demo will be released on, first being available on PC, followed by Android and iOS once we manage to port it properly.

The original idea was to make this a VR game since we're interested in seeing how walking around small planets would play in virtual environment. But that part is postponed for now because we do not currently own any VR headsets to test on.
